            <h1>First Solar, Cabot help pace energy gains</h1>
            <p><p class="leadin">NEW YORK (MarketWatch) — Energy stocks closed higher Tuesday as oil prices strengthened and the broader equities market advanced, with shares of First Solar Inc. posting solid gains.</p>
<p>The Energy Select Sector SPDR Fund <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="XLE">XLE</span> rose 1.1%. The fund contains energy stocks included in the S&#038;P 500 Index <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="SPX">SPX</span>. </p>
</p>
<p>Among that group, First Solar <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="FSLR">FSLR</span> rose 4.9% in the latest in a series of big moves by the company’s stock. <a href='http://www.marketwatch.com/Story/Story/?guid={168714E2-F907-11E1-A4AB-002128049AD6}'>See story on First Solar’s turnaround strategy.</a></p>
<p>Separately, First Solar said it struck a power-purchase agreement with Pacific Gas &#038; Electric Co. <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="PCG">PCG</span> for 72 megawatts of solar power from two plants under development in central California. The agreement will provide electricity for about 24,000 homes, the company said.</p>
<p>Also making gains at the closing bell, Cabot Oil &#038; Gas Corp. <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="COG">COG</span> rose 5.7%, Alpha Natural Resources Inc. <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="ANR">ANR</span> added 7.7% and Denbury Resources Inc.  <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="DNR">DNR</span> advanced by 4.6%. </p>
<p>Helping set the trading tone in energy stocks, crude-oil futures <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="CLV2">CLV2</span> rose to close above $97 a barrel on speculation about a new stimulus plan from the Federal Reserve ahead of a meeting by the U.S. central bank this week. <a href='http://www.marketwatch.com/Story/Story/?guid={BD02AB54-FBCF-11E1-BAF9-002128049AD6}'>See Futures Movers for more on oil prices.</a></p>
<p>The Dow Jones Industrial Average <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="DJIA">DJIA</span> rose 69 points, with blue-chip energy components Exxon Mobil Corp. <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="XOM">XOM</span> and Chevron Corp. <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="CVX">CVX</span> up by about 0.2% each.</p>
<p>Plains Exploration &#038; Production Inc. <span class="blankTicker" data-widget="dj.ticker" data-ticker-name="PXP">PXP</span> stabilized, adding 1.6% after a nearly 11% loss in the previous session on its $6 billion acquisition plan for oil fields in the Gulf of Mexico. <a href='http://www.marketwatch.com/Story/Story/?guid={15B7FF5C-FB3E-11E1-BAF9-002128049AD6}'>See story on Plains’s acquisition.</a></p>
</p>
